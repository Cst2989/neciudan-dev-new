Neciu Dan - Tech Lead | Software Engineer | Speaker | Writer

    Twitter Youtube Linkedin Github Medium

Neciu Dan is a software engineer with a predominant focus on the front-end. He shares a passionate relationship with JavaScript and has professional experience with all of the Big Three Frameworks. Among them, he has a special fondness for React, despite his company's preference for Vue in many projects.

Constantly striving for excellence, Neciu Dan endeavours to strike the perfect balance. He aims to deliver visually stunning user interfaces while also ensuring rapid and valuable product outcomes. At the same time, he is committed to producing the cleanest and most easily maintainable code possible.

In a recent endeavor, Neciu Dan co-founded an ed-tech startup, stepping into the role of a technical co-founder. This new venture has allowed him to channel his expertise and passion for technology into the realm of education. Embracing the challenges of the startup world, he is dedicated to leveraging his technical acumen to revolutionize the educational landscape and create meaningful impacts in the sector.
